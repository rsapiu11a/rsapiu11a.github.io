## Why Vim feels different

Vim is not just an editor — it’s a **language for editing text**.

Once muscle memory kicks in, you stop thinking about *how* to edit and focus on *what* to write.

## Modal editing

Vim separates:
- **navigation**
- **editing**
- **inserting**

This reduces cognitive load dramatically.

```vim
dd        " delete line
ciw       " change inner word
:%s/foo/bar/g
